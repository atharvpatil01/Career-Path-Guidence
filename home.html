Choose the right stream based on your interests and goals

Science Stream

For students interested in engineering, medicine, research, and technology.

  • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology
  • Leads to: Engineering, Medical, Research
  • Exams: JEE, NEET, KVPY
High analytical skills required
📊

Commerce Stream

For students interested in business, finance, accounting, and economics.

  • Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics
  • Leads to: CA, MBA, Banking, Business
  • Exams: CA Foundation, CS, CMA
Good with numbers & logic
🎨

Arts / Humanities

For creative minds interested in social sciences, law, design, and media.

  • History, Political Science, Psychology
  • Leads to: Law, Journalism, Design, Civil Services
  • Exams: CLAT, NID, UPSC
Creative & expressive minds
